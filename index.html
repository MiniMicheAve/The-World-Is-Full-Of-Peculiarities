Billie Eilish, born on December 18th, 2001 in LA, California, is an american singer/songwriter and is the second and youngest artist in Grammy history to win all 4 general field categories- Record of The Year, Album of The Year, Song of The Year, as well as Best New Artist- in the same year. She is ranked 15th most popular artist in the world and continues to rise in fame.
